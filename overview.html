Overview

Great Thandoria, often referred to simply as Thandoria, is a prominent empire located in a diverse and historically rich region. Tasmania serves as the national capital, housing the Imperial Assembly and Tannis Palace, the residence of the Emperor. With a population of approximately 92 million, the empire celebrates its cultural diversity through festivals and traditions. The official language is Thandorian, and the nation spans 400,000 km², featuring fertile plains, rugged mountains, and scenic coastlines.

History

The Great Restoration War (1825–1850)

The Empire of Great Thandoria was established following the Great Restoration War, which unified a fragmented region of feudal states.

Pre-War Conditions

  • Political Instability: A chaotic patchwork of feudal states, constant warfare, and shifting alliances created widespread disillusionment.
  • Economic Stagnation: Trade hampered by tariffs, poor infrastructure, famine, and poverty were common.

Emergence of Emperor Thandor I

Thandor I from Tasmania unified the region, forming alliances and leading innovative military campaigns.

Key Milestones

  • Battle of Lunatia (1836): Decisive victory showcasing Thandor's tactical genius.
  • Siege of Eldermere (1839): Captured rival capitals, forcing lords to reconsider allegiances.
  • Treaty of Unification (1850): Officially established the empire, marking the First Thandorian Golden Age.

Previous Dynasties/Empires

  • The Velmorian Empire (300–800 CE): Mountain state, advanced metallurgy, rich culture.
  • The Chevalan Confederacy (800–1200 CE): Agricultural coalition, democratic assemblies, agricultural innovation.
  • The Tasmanian Kingdom (1200–1500 CE): Coastal naval power, thriving trade, education and arts.
  • The Almirian Trade Alliance (1500–1800 CE): Maritime city-states, commerce-focused, dissolved leading to the Great Restoration War.

Geography

The empire spans 400,000 km² with diverse terrains. It is divided into four main states:

Climate

Varies from temperate coasts to alpine highlands, supporting rich biodiversity.

Culture

A rich tapestry from diverse ethnic backgrounds, flourishing arts, literature, and education.

Cultural Highlights

  • Annual Festivals: Unity Day, Harvest Festival, Festival of Lights.
  • Culinary Fame: Globally renowned cheeses and fusion cuisine from Cheval Plains.
  • Arts and Literature: Classical music, fine arts, and historical literature.

National Pledge: Reflects unity, resilience, and commitment to the empire.

Politics

Constitutional monarchy with a representative democracy. The Emperor is ceremonial; power lies with the elected Chancellor and Imperial Assembly.

Government Structure

  • Legislature: Bicameral Imperial Assembly (elected lower house + appointed upper house).
  • Judiciary: Independent courts under the Imperial Charter of 1952.
  • Elections: Every five years, including votes by expatriates.

Political Parties

  • People's Justice Movement (PJM) – ruling party, social equality, civil rights.
  • Progressive Alliance (PA) – coalition partner, environmentalism, social justice.
  • National Sovereignty Party (NSP) – main opposition, nationalism, traditional values.

Economy

Diverse and robust, transitioning from agrarian to industrial prowess with strategic state involvement.

Key Industries

  • Manufacturing: Advanced machinery, vehicles, innovation-focused.
  • Agriculture: Dairy, wheat, specialty cheeses.
  • Energy: Uranium mining, nuclear supply, renewable investment.
  • Tourism: Scenic destinations, festivals, cultural exchange.
