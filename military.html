Great Thandoria — Military Overview

The Imperial Military safeguards Great Thandoria’s sovereignty, protects its citizens, and projects national power. Below is an authoritative overview of command structures, branch commands, major assets, strategic forces, and procurement.

High Command

Top leadership & strategic direction
Supreme Commander of the Armed Forces
General Maximilian Adler (Titan)

A distinguished 4‑star general with extensive combat and diplomatic experience; responsible for strategic direction of all armed forces.

Chief of Staff, Army
General Sylvia Cross (Valor)

Renowned for logistical expertise and operational planning; led modernization of army tactics and force readiness efforts.

Commander, Strategic Operations
General Corvin Rausch (Overwatch)

Oversees nuclear, intercontinental, and strategic deterrence systems; reports directly to Supreme Commander for launch authorization.

Branch Command Structures

Operational commands and theatre leadership
Imperial Army Command Structure

Four theater-level Army Commands manage frontline operations, reserves, training, and garrison duties.

Imperial Air Force Command Structure

Northern, Southern, Central, and Special Operations Air Commands manage air defense, strategic bombing, and rapid deployment forces.

Imperial Navy Command Structure

Northern, Southern, and Central Fleet Commands handle surface and submarine warfare, amphibious operations, and port defense.

Special Operations Forces Command Structure

Special Operations Forces include Shadow Company, Steel Vipers, Ghost Team, and Phantom Corps with global rapid-response capability.
