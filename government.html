Government Structure of Great Thandoria

Executive Branch

President of Great Thandoria (POGT)

Chief executive, commander-in-chief, sets national agenda

Cabinet (Secretaries)

Position Office/Body Details
Vice President (VPOGT) Executive Branch Leader of Imperial Assembly
Sec. of Defense (SECDEF) Department of Defense Oversees Defense Department, principal defense/war policy advisor.
Sec. of State (SECSTATE) Department of State Chief foreign affairs adviser, carries out President's foreign policies.
Sec. of Treasury Department of Treasury Maintains economy, fosters growth, manages government finances.
Sec. of Interior Department of the Interior Manages federal lands through agencies like National Park Service.
Sec. of Justice Department of Justice Represents the nation in legal matters, advises President and departments.
Sec. of Agriculture Department of Agriculture Directs policy and programs for food, farming, and rural development.
Sec. of Commerce Department of Commerce Principal advisor to President on commerce matters.
Sec. of Labor Department of Labor Advises President on labor policies, workplace conditions, wages, and rights.
Sec. of Health Department of Health Leads HHS, advises on health policy and programs.
Sec. of Transportation Department of Transportation Oversees national transportation policy and promotes intermodal transportation.
Sec. of Education Department of Education Leads federal education policy, advises President.
Sec. of Homeland Security Department of Homeland Security Oversees counter-terrorism, border security, immigration enforcement, and cybersecurity.
Chief of Staff Executive Branch Strategic advisor and executor for President, manages operations and liaison duties.
Legislative Branch

Imperial Assembly

Unicameral legislature, 300 seats; passes laws, approves budget, oversees executive

Position Office/Body Details
Speaker of the Assembly Legislative Branch Presides over Assembly, sets legislative agenda.
Majority Leader Legislative Branch Leads majority party, coordinates bills and voting.
Minority Leader Legislative Branch Leads minority party, provides checks and debate.
Committees Legislative Branch Specialized committees: Finance, Defense, Health, Education, etc.
Judiciary Branch

Supreme Tribunal of Thandoria

Highest Court, interprets laws, ensures constitutionality, oversees lower courts

Position Office/Body Details
Chief Justice Judiciary Leads Supreme Tribunal, oversees judicial decisions.
Associate Justices Judiciary Serve on Supreme Tribunal, hear cases and issue rulings.
Lower Courts Judiciary Regional and local courts handling daily judicial matters.
Bureaucracy / Agencies

National Departments & Agencies

Implement policies and programs; report to President or Cabinet

Position Office/Body Details
Department of Defense Agency Provide military forces to deter war and ensure national security.
Department of State Agency Protect and promote security, prosperity, and democratic values internationally.
Department of Treasury Agency Maintain a strong economy and create job opportunities.
Department of the Interior Agency Protect and manage natural resources and cultural heritage.
Department of Justice Agency Uphold the rule of law, protect civil rights, and ensure safety.
Department of Agriculture Agency Support farmers, ensure food safety, and promote agriculture.
Department of Commerce Agency Create conditions for economic growth and opportunity.
Department of Labor Agency Promote welfare of workers, improve conditions, ensure rights.
Department of Health Agency Enhance health and well-being, oversee medical and public health services.
Department of Transportation Agency Manage federal transportation projects, safety regulations, and policy.
Department of Education Agency Promote student achievement, global competitiveness, and access to education.
Department of Homeland Security Agency Secure the nation, counter threats, manage borders, and ensure cybersecurity.
